About Me

A Creative Night Owl

I am a Toronto-based web developer with a graphic design background.

When I first discovered the web, I was intrigued to learn how it worked. Quickly, I fell in love with it. My time went into creating websites and playing flash games.

After a decade of calling it a hobby, I took the opportunity to turn it into a profession.

Now, as a graphic design graduate, I have a deeper understanding of layout, color, animation, and typography, further pushing my skills as a developer.
